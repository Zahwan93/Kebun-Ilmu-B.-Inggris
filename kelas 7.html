B. Example Conversation

Percakapan dua teman yang sedang berjumpa dan berpisah:

A: Good morning, Budi!

B: Morning, Sita!

A: How are you?

B: I’m fine, thank you. And you?

A: I’m great!

B: I have to go now. Goodbye!

A: See you later!

A: Selamat pagi, Budi!

B: Pagi, Sita!

A: Apa kabar?

B: Aku baik-baik saja, terima kasih. Kamu?

A: Aku juga baik!

B: Aku harus pergi sekarang. Sampai jumpa!

A: Sampai nanti!

2. The Fried Rice is Spicy

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Students are able to identify the taste of food and drink.
  • Students are able to mention the taste of food and drink.
  • Students are able to write sentences about the taste of food and drink.

A. Vocabulary Table (Tabel Kosakata)

Kosakata penting terkait nasi goreng dan rasa pedas:

English Word Meaning (Arti)
Fried riceNasi goreng
SpicyPedas
RiceNasi
DeliciousLezat / Enak
ChiliCabai
GarlicBawang putih
EggTelur
Soy sauceKecap
PlatePiring
SpatulaSpatula (alat masak)
SweetManis
SourAsam
HotPanas
ColdDingin
BitterPahit
SaltyAsin
TasteRasa

B. Example Conversation (Contoh Percakapan)

Percakapan dua orang yang sedang makan nasi goreng:

A: What are you eating?

B: I’m eating fried rice.

A: It smells spicy!

B: Yes, the fried rice is spicy but delicious!

A: Where did you buy it?

B: I bought it at the food stall near the park.

A: Kamu sedang makan apa?

B: Aku sedang makan nasi goreng.

A: Wah, baunya pedas!

B: Iya, nasi gorengnya pedas tapi enak!

A: Kamu beli di mana?

B: Aku beli di warung dekat taman.

C. Example Narrative (Contoh Narasi Pendek)

Teks narasi tentang pengalaman makan nasi goreng:

Last night, I was very hungry after studying. I went to a small warung near my house and ordered fried rice.

The fried rice was hot and very spicy. It had chili, garlic, egg, and sweet soy sauce. Even though it was spicy, I really liked it.

I finished the whole plate and felt full and happy.

Tadi malam, saya sangat lapar setelah belajar. Saya pergi ke warung kecil dekat rumah dan memesan nasi goreng.

Nasi gorengnya panas dan sangat pedas. Ada cabai, bawang putih, telur, dan kecap manis. Meskipun pedas, saya sangat menyukainya.

Saya menghabiskan seluruh piring dan merasa kenyang dan senang.
