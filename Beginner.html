1. Self-Introduction

A. Sapaan Dasar (Greetings)

Berikut adalah beberapa ungkapan sapaan yang umum digunakan dalam Bahasa Inggris:

Hello

Hi

Good morning

Good afternoon

Good night

B. Memperkenalkan Diri (Introducing Yourself)

Gunakan kalimat-kalimat ini saat memperkenalkan diri:

My name is...

I am from...

I am a student

C. Menanyakan Kabar (Asking and Answering "How are you?")

Tanya kabar dan jawabannya bisa menggunakan kalimat berikut:

How are you?

I’m fine, thank you

D. Perpisahan (Farewell)

Ucapan perpisahan yang umum:

Goodbye

See you

Take care

E. Speaking Practice (Latihan Berbicara)

Latih pengucapanmu dengan membaca kalimat-kalimat berikut dengan suara keras:

Hello, my name is Andi.

I am 13 years old.

I am from Indonesia.

I am a student at SMP Merdeka.

My hobby is playing football.

F. Example Dialogue (Contoh Dialog)

Contoh percakapan saat bertemu teman baru:

A: Hello!

B: Hi!

A: What’s your name?

B: My name is Rina. What about you?

A: I’m Ali. Nice to meet you!

B: Nice to meet you too.

G. Example Text (Cerita Perkenalan Diri Sebagai Murid Baru)

Berikut adalah contoh teks narasi tentang seorang murid baru yang memperkenalkan dirinya:

My name is Dimas. Today is my first day at SMP Harapan Bangsa. I just moved to this city from Bandung.

I am 13 years old. I live in a small house near the school with my parents and my sister.

At the beginning of the class, my teacher asked me to introduce myself. I said, "Hello everyone, my name is Dimas. I come from Bandung. My hobbies are playing football and reading comics. I hope we can be good friends."

All of the students smiled at me and welcomed me warmly. I was very happy to meet my new classmates.

Nama saya Dimas. Hari ini adalah hari pertama saya di SMP Harapan Bangsa. Saya baru saja pindah ke kota ini dari Bandung.

Saya berumur 13 tahun. Saya tinggal di sebuah rumah kecil dekat sekolah bersama orang tua dan adik perempuan saya.

Di awal pelajaran, guru saya meminta saya untuk memperkenalkan diri. Saya berkata, "Halo semuanya, nama saya Dimas. Saya berasal dari Bandung. Hobi saya adalah bermain sepak bola dan membaca komik. Saya harap kita bisa menjadi teman yang baik."

Semua siswa tersenyum kepada saya dan menyambut saya dengan hangat. Saya sangat senang bertemu dengan teman-teman baru saya.

Nama saya Sinta. Saya pergi ke sekolah setiap hari dengan sepeda. Sekolah saya tidak terlalu jauh dari rumah. Saya paling suka pelajaran IPA dan Bahasa Inggris. Setelah pulang sekolah, saya membantu ibu memasak makan malam dan belajar bersama adik perempuan saya.

Last Sunday, my family and I visited my grandmother in the village. The weather was nice and sunny. We walked around the rice fields and played with the animals. My grandmother cooked delicious food for us and we ate together under the tree.

Terjemahan yang benar:
Hari Minggu lalu, saya dan keluarga mengunjungi nenek saya di desa. Cuacanya cerah dan menyenangkan. Kami berjalan-jalan di sekitar sawah dan bermain dengan hewan-hewan. Nenek saya memasak makanan lezat untuk kami dan kami makan bersama di bawah pohon.

Setiap pagi, Sinta membantu ibunya menyapu halaman dan menyiram tanaman. Setelah itu, ia bersiap-siap pergi ke sekolah. Sinta sangat rajin dan selalu datang tepat waktu.

Correct translation:
Every morning, Sinta helps her mother sweep the yard and water the plants. After that, she gets ready to go to school. Sinta is very diligent and always arrives on time.

Pada hari Sabtu, Riko dan teman-temannya pergi ke perpustakaan untuk belajar bersama. Mereka membaca buku, berdiskusi, dan saling membantu mengerjakan tugas. Hari itu sangat menyenangkan bagi mereka.

Correct translation:
On Saturday, Riko and his friends went to the library to study together. They read books, had discussions, and helped each other do their assignments. It was a very fun day for them.

Liburan semester lalu, keluarga kami pergi ke rumah paman di Bandung. Kami berangkat pagi-pagi sekali agar tidak terjebak macet. Di perjalanan, kami melihat pemandangan yang sangat indah, seperti pegunungan, sawah, dan sungai. Setibanya di sana, paman dan bibi menyambut kami dengan hangat. Selama beberapa hari, kami berjalan-jalan ke berbagai tempat wisata, mencicipi makanan khas Sunda, dan berfoto bersama. Liburan ini sangat berkesan karena kami bisa berkumpul dan bersenang-senang bersama keluarga besar.

Correct translation:
Last semester holiday, our family went to our uncle’s house in Bandung. We left very early in the morning to avoid traffic jams. On the way, we saw beautiful sceneries such as mountains, rice fields, and rivers. When we arrived, our uncle and aunt welcomed us warmly. For several days, we visited many tourist attractions, tasted Sundanese traditional food, and took pictures together. This holiday was very memorable because we could gather and have fun with the whole family.

Perkenalkan, nama saya Aulia Rahmawati. Saya lahir di Surabaya pada tanggal 15 April 2009. Sekarang saya tinggal di kota Malang bersama orang tua dan adik saya. Saya adalah siswa kelas 8 di SMP Nusantara. Hobi saya adalah membaca buku cerita dan bermain musik. Saya bercita-cita menjadi seorang penulis terkenal.

Correct translation:
Let me introduce myself. My name is Aulia Rahmawati. I was born in Surabaya on April 15th, 2009. Now I live in Malang with my parents and my younger sibling. I am an eighth-grade student at SMP Nusantara. My hobbies are reading storybooks and playing music. I want to become a famous writer in the future.

Pada hari Minggu lalu, kami sekeluarga menghadiri pesta pernikahan sepupu di kota. Di sana, orang tua saya bertemu dengan teman lamanya yang sudah lama tidak berjumpa. Mereka terlihat sangat senang bisa bertemu kembali. Setelah mengobrol sebentar, orang tua saya mulai memperkenalkan kami satu per satu. Ayah memperkenalkan saya, adik, dan ibu kepada temannya. Ia menyebutkan nama kami, umur, tempat tinggal, sekolah, dan pekerjaan. Saya merasa bangga diperkenalkan dan bisa berbicara sedikit dengan teman ayah saya. Suasana pesta pun menjadi lebih hangat dan penuh canda tawa.

Correct translation:
Last Sunday, my family and I attended our cousin's wedding in the city. There, my parents met one of their old friends whom they hadn’t seen in a long time. They looked very happy to see each other again. After chatting for a while, my parents started to introduce us one by one. My father introduced me, my sibling, and my mother to his friend. He mentioned our names, ages, where we live, schools, and jobs. I felt proud to be introduced and was able to talk a little with my father’s friend. The atmosphere at the wedding became warmer and full of laughter.

Topik: Family

Q: How many people are there in your family?

A: There are five people in my family: my father, mother, brother, sister, and me.

Q: Does your family live in a house or an apartment?

A: We live in a house in the countryside.

Q: What does your father do?

A: My father is a doctor. He works at the local hospital.

Q: How old is your mother?

A: She is 40 years old, 1 year younger than my father.

Q: Do you have any siblings? What's his/her name?

A: Yes, I do. I have 1 elder brother, David, and 1 younger sister, Mary.

Q: Are you the oldest amongst your brothers and sisters?

A: No, I’m not. I’m the second child in my family.

Q: What does your mother/father like?

A: My father likes playing football and my mother likes cooking.

Q: Do your parents let you stay out late?

A: Of course not. They always ask me to get home before 10 pm each night.

Q: Do you stay with your parents?

A: Right now, no, but I used to.

Q: Does your family usually have dinner together?

A: Yes, we do. My mom always prepares delicious meals for us.

Topik: Restaurant

Q: How often do you eat out? Who do you go with?

A: I often eat out on weekends, when I hang out with my friends.

Q: What restaurant do you usually visit?

A: Well, there are not many restaurants in my neighborhood, so my best choice is the deli in convenience stores like the Circle K, Mini‑Stop, B‑smart.

A: I’m interested in Asian food, Western food is not my thing.

A: It’s not very expensive, just around $5 for each meal.

A: Yes, I do, especially on cold days.

A: Yes, they are. Most of them are really helpful.

A: Yes, at least once, when I was in my friend’s wedding party.

A: Yes, I am. I’m on diet now, so this really matters to me.

A: Yes, they are. The youth in my country are big fans of fast food.

Topik: Hobbies

Q: What is your hobby?

A: I like playing sports, especially swimming.

Q: Is your hobby common in your country?

A: Yes, kids, adults, and senior citizens all like swimming in my country.

Q: Is your hobby the same as when you were a child?

A: Yes, I was fond of swimming when I was a little girl.

Q: When did you start practicing that hobby?

A: I started swimming when I was 5 years old.

Q: Is there anybody in your family who you share your hobby with?

A: My dad, he taught me how to swim.

Q: How much time do you spend on your hobby?

A: I usually go to the swimming pool twice a week.

Q: What benefits do you get from it?

A: Swimming is a really good workout. It helps me keep fit and chill out after a long day working.

Q: Have you ever heard of someone having a very unusual hobby? What was it?

A: Yes, my cousin loves tattooing vehicles. He puts stickers everywhere on his car.

Q: What do you think about someone having weird hobbies?

A: I don’t think any hobby is weird. It’s just what you like to do, so I don’t judge others.

Q: What hobbies are usually expensive in your country?

A: Playing golf is a really expensive hobby. Only the rich can afford to buy golf supplies.

Soal 26–30: Dengarkan percakapan berikut

Topik: A School

A: What school did you go to?

B: I went to Millennium High School, founded in 1999.

A: Where is the school located?

B: It’s located in New York City, United States.

A: Do you like the architecture of the school?

B: Yes, I do. The architecture is not really impressive, but I like it that the building’s architects left plenty of space for lounging.

A: What are the teachers like?

B: Most of the teachers there are helpful and friendly. I especially like Mr. Mike, my Physics teacher.

A: How long have you spent there?

B: I have spent 3 years of upper secondary school there.

A: Is that a single-sex school?

B: No, it isn’t. This is a unisex school.

A: Do you like the school uniform?

B: We don’t wear uniforms at school, actually.

A: Why do you enjoy the time there?

B: Although I had to deal with quite a heavy workload, I enjoy the relaxing atmosphere when hanging out with friends in the cafeteria there.

A: What important lesson did you learn from school?

B: I learned how to work in a group in which there are many friends coming from different cultures. That’s the lesson of cooperation.

A: Will you recommend that school to others?

B: Yes, of course. I’m proud to recommend Millennium High School to anyone who is searching for a good place to learn.

Soal 28: Who is the speaker’s favorite teacher?

Soal 30: Does the speaker recommend the school?

Topik: Technological Advancements

A: Are you a tech and gadget lover?

Apakah kamu penyuka teknologi dan gadget?

B: Yes, I am. I love shopping for new gadgets.

Ya. Saya suka berbelanja gadget baru.

A: What is an important technological advancement you know?

Apa kemajuan teknologi penting yang kamu ketahui?

B: That’s the emergence of online payment systems like Credit Cards or PayPal thanks to the development of the Internet, I think.

Menurut saya, munculnya sistem pembayaran online seperti Kartu Kredit atau PayPal berkat perkembangan internet.

A: What do you use it for?

Untuk apa kamu menggunakannya?

B: I use these services to do shopping online. Now I can book flight tickets in advance without the need to go to the agency.

Saya menggunakan layanan ini untuk belanja online. Sekarang saya bisa memesan tiket pesawat lebih awal tanpa harus ke agen perjalanan.

A: How long have you been using it?

Sudah berapa lama kamu menggunakannya?

B: I’ve been using these services for around 3 years.

Saya sudah menggunakan layanan ini selama sekitar 3 tahun.

A: Does it make your life easier?

Apakah itu membuat hidupmu lebih mudah?

B: Sure. It’s fast, simple and convenient.

Tentu. Ini cepat, sederhana, dan praktis.

A: How did it change your life?

Bagaimana itu mengubah hidupmu?

B: Well, I can enjoy my life more. I can do a plenty of things online like shopping, transferring money, booking tickets, and so on.

Saya bisa lebih menikmati hidup. Saya bisa melakukan banyak hal secara online seperti belanja, mentransfer uang, memesan tiket, dan sebagainya.

A: How often do you use it?

Seberapa sering kamu menggunakannya?

B: At least once a week.

Setidaknya sekali seminggu.

A: What are other technological advancements you know?

Apa kemajuan teknologi lain yang kamu ketahui?

B: There are many: electricity, smartphone, the Internet, robot, and so on.

Banyak sekali: listrik, ponsel pintar, internet, robot, dan lain-lain.
